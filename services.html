Our Work

At PESAT, we engage in multiple activities to protect and serve nature and animals:

  • Providing food and shelter to stray animals like dogs, cows, and birds.
  • Organizing free vaccination and health camps for animals.
  • Planting trees and creating awareness for environmental protection.
  • Helping economically weaker sections by providing essential services.
  • Running social media campaigns for public awareness about animal rights and nature preservation.
