What is PESAT?

PESAT (Protection of Environment, Society, and Animal Trust) is a non-profit initiative started to serve the voiceless animals, nurture the environment by planting trees, and help economically weaker sections of society. Our aim is to create a harmonious coexistence among animals, humans, and nature.
