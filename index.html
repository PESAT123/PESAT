About PESAT

PESAT (Paryavaran, Jeev Aur Samajik Seva Trust) is a non-profit initiative started with a strong passion to serve voiceless animals like cows, dogs, and birds, protect the environment through tree plantation, and support underprivileged and economically weaker sections of society. We believe that every life matters, and our mission is to create a world where compassion and care reach every corner—whether it's a barking street dog or a struggling human.

The movement begins with small, heartfelt actions like feeding animals, rescuing injured birds, and planting saplings. With more support and donations, we aim to expand our scope and reach. Our volunteers will work actively in rural and urban areas. In the beginning, we will help where we can, and with time, the mission will grow into a large-scale change for good.

Every donation we receive will directly go towards the welfare of animals, plantations, and social work. A small percentage might support the operation and needs of our full-time volunteers, who dedicate their lives to this cause.

Let’s build PESAT together—a voice for the voiceless and a shield for the earth.
